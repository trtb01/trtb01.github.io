Appendix

Here's a quick list of all of the specific options in Steam to filter out all that sex shit (or whatever else you're not comfy with!):

Mature Content Filtering

  • General Mature Content: Topics that may not be appropriate for all audiences.

  • Frequent Violence or Gore: Content featuring extreme violence.

  • Some Nudity or Sexual Content: Content where nudity/sexual themes are not the primary focus.

  • Frequent Nudity or Sexual Content: Content primarily featuring nudity or sexual themes.

Adult Only Sexual Content

  • Adult Only Sexual Content: A specific checkbox for explicit content intended for adults only (requires age affirmation).

Community Content Preferences (Chat Filtering)

  • Language Preferences: Options to filter strong profanity and slurs (Filter both, Allow profanity but filter slurs, or Do not filter).

  • User Group Preferences: Checkbox to "Do not filter text from my Steam Friends."

  • Additional Custom Filtered Words: Input fields to "Always filter" or "Never filter" specific words.

Tags & Ignored Products

  • Tags To Exclude: Input field to filter out games by specific tags (e.g., Horror, Anime).

  • Ignored Products: View and manage specific titles you have manually set to "Ignore."
